My Music Portfolio

I play Violin, Piano, and Drums. I studied Violin and Music Theory in the Istanbul University State Conservatory. I have participated in over 40 concerts in my 5 years of study in Paris.

I am an administrator in AMEVS where we primarily play Haitian classical music to raise funds for a children's orchestra in Haiti, and a member of the Violin 1 section in OSIUP (Paris Inter-University Symphony Orchestra).
